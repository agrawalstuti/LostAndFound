About Us

    Just over a year ago, we witnessed a sorrowful calamity of losing people at a momentous event The Kumbh Mela at Prayagraj, Uttar Pradesh which is billed as largest gathering of humanity on earth, with 110 million people expected to attend over 49 days. But for the thousands who gets lost among the crowds, help is at hand.

Most people know the heart-sinking feeling of losing someone in a crowded place. Imagine the feeling of being lost at a large gathering. It’s a scene so dramatic, and so common, that it’s a theme in many Bollywood movies- families who attend the Kumbh are separated and then reunited decades later.This scenario is not only can be seen at this gigantic event but can also be seen at any place which encompasses huge gathering.

Similarly, just think about losing an essential commodity at such crowded gathering or infact at any place. We can’t afford to lose our important stuff in today’s running life and if we lose them, we want them back as soon as possible.

Our Vision!!

We will aim to help maximum number of people, so that we can create a community of people who would want to help others in finding their lost belongings or their family members

Our main aim is to make this website global so that it can be used all around the world and it can be done only when people will feel honest and be kind toward other people.

As we have seen many people don’t bother much when they find some items which are lost so they either keep it thinking who will find the real owner or leave it there. It has also been known that during any calamity or chaos if the younger ones or old aged people are lost who barely remember their address, it takes a month or more by the police to find them.

We want people to find their old aged family member or younger children in less time. This will also help to generate the feeling of honesty in every person that they might want to help other people in reaching to their belongings because they don’t need to reach to police they just have to login to this website.

HOW WILL WE WORK?

Lost and found does not involve any third party, whatever matching is done here is taking place with the help of OpenCv.Users may sign in and feed the details of any lost person or entity that they want to find and they can also feed the details of any found person or entity about which they have no idea that to whom the found object belongs to or the found person is related to. To find the lost people, we would be performing image mapping between the images of found people and images of lost people and vice-versa. Similarly to find the lost item we would be performing feature mapping between the images of found entities and images of lost entities and vice-versa. Later the image matching we would also do text matching between the descriptions of lost entity and found entity. If the final result of image and text matching is TRUE then final verification would be done by admin and then users are notified with the result and other details, but if the result is not true, then input details get stored in our record for future process
